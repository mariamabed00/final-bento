To make Black Raspberry Ice Cream (Dairy-Free):

1. Blend 2 cups of black raspberries until smooth.
2. Strain the mixture to remove the seeds.
3. Whisk together 1 can of full-fat coconut milk, 1/4 cup maple syrup or agave nectar, and 1 teaspoon vanilla extract.
4. Combine the strained raspberry puree with the coconut milk mixture.
5. Adjust sweetness to taste.
6. Churn the mixture in an ice cream maker until soft-serve consistency.
7. Transfer to an airtight container and freeze for 2-3 hours until firm.
8. Allow the ice cream to soften briefly before serving.
9. Enjoy!
Here are the steps to make a dairy-free peanut butter and jelly smoothie:

1. Peel and slice a ripe banana.
2. In a blender, combine the sliced banana, frozen mixed berries, peanut butter, non-dairy milk, and chia seeds (if using).
3. Blend the ingredients on high speed until smooth and creamy. If the mixture is too thick, you can add more non-dairy milk to achieve the desired consistency.
4. Taste the smoothie and adjust the sweetness if needed. You can add a drizzle of honey or a sweetener of your choice if desired.
5. Pour the smoothie into a glass and serve immediately.
Enjoy your delicious dairy-free peanut butter and jelly smoothie!
